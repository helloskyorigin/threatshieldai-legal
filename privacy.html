Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ThreatShield AI handles your data, scans, and app activity. Please read it carefully before using the app.

1. Information We Process

ThreatShield AI processes the content you choose to scan, such as messages and links, to detect potential scam, phishing, malicious, or suspicious activity.

2. How We Use Data

We use scan data only to generate security analysis results, improve detection quality, and provide relevant warnings or recommendations inside the app.

3. Storage and Retention

Some scan history may be stored locally or in the app’s configured backend so you can review past results and app usage. We keep only what is needed for the app to function.

4. Third-Party Services

ThreatShield AI may use third-party security services to check links for known threats. These services are used only for security analysis and app functionality.

5. Permissions

If the app requests permissions such as notifications, they are used only for app features and user experience. We do not access permissions unrelated to the app’s purpose.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared only when required for app operation, security analysis, legal compliance, or service functionality.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the data we process, but no digital system can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any new version will be posted here with an updated date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the app team through the official support channel or website.
